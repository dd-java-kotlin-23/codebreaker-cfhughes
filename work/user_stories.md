in order to feel more accomplished when I win

As an advertiser who is targeting busy professionals
I want to purchase ad time in apps that people are interested in
So that I can show them short videos when I know they are paying attention

As the owner of a retirement home
I want to provide puzzles to my patients and see their progress
So that I can have information about their brain health over time

As a data broker who mines the data of smartphone users
I want to know how smart people are and how good their memory is
So that I can sell that information along with other information about them
